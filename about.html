Esports

Electronic sports, aka Esports, are organized video game competitions between players, participating individually or in teams. It gives spectators the experience of professional sports instead of physical events. Esports is a way where people interact with each other through a digital environment. Additionally, instead of only online events, Esports also consists of LAN events, where everyone can interact in person, a competitive environment among players and spectators, like in any physical sport.

What is Esports Society

E-sports society is one of the most active and engaging society among all Educational Institutes. In the past few years there has been enormous rise in participation and competitiveness standards in esports at IIT Kanpur, making the E-sports society an integral part of the Games & Sports Council. The Society envisions to pro-vide a platform for all eSports enthusiasts to participate and interact by organizing tournaments, workshops, podcasts, sessions and much more.
